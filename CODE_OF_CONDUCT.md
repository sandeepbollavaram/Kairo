# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project follows a simple principle: **be respectful, be technical, be honest.**

- Critique ideas, not people. Strong technical disagreement is welcome and encouraged.
- No harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Assume good faith in others; argue from evidence and reasoning.

## Scope

Applies to all project spaces — issues, discussions, and any other official channel.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to the maintainer privately. The maintainer may remove
comments, lock threads, or ban accounts for behavior deemed inappropriate, threatening,
or harmful. Decisions are final during the project's pre-1.0 phase.

Adapted in spirit from the Contributor Covenant.
